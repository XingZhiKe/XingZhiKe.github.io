一、产品目的

首先，我们都希望能够做一个健康的人、做一个身材好的人。
但是，由于懒惰、繁杂事情等众多原因，导致我们经常无法保持健康的饮食和良好的身材。
所以导致我们与心中的自己越来越远，并因此而产生肥胖且越来越严重。

二、产品介绍

为支持用户的使用，下面对产品进行详细的说明。

2.1 用户引导

炎症检测

炎症检测共有12个步骤，分别是就12个问题进行用户调查：

  1. You are emotionally unstable sometimes you feel very happy, and other times feeling very sad.
  2. Do you have constipation or diarrhea?
  3. Do you have dry or unusually oily skin, or issues such as redness, inflammation, patches, itching, or rashes?
  4. You are more prone to catching a cold.
  5. You often feel pain in your muscles or joints.
  6. Even after 8 hours of sleep, you still wake up feeling exhausted.
  7. You feel drained in the afternoon, sometimes don’t get your energy back until later in the evening.
  8. You start to feel completely worn out around 3 PM, and sometimes you don’t feel better until later in the evening.
  9. Do you often have trouble sleeping or suffer from insomnia?
  10. Your ring is getting tighter, your face or your legs may become swollen.
  11. You have difficulty breathing smoothly and fully.
  12. Your eyes are red, swollen, or itchy.

基本信息收集

通过这些基本信息来进行分析，对用户的体重控制目标进行分析

  1. What is your gender?
  2. Your date of birth?
  3. How tall are you?
  4. What is your goal?
  5. Your current weight?
  6. Your target weight
  7. Choose the progress
  8. Your Weekly Exercise Level

对用户文字、语音输入的食品信息进行基于AI的分析，对食物的属性、营养成分、炎症属性的进行分析。
